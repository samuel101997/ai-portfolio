Version 2.8 | Effective: January 1, 2026
Internal Document - Customer Support Reference

====================================================================
SECTION 1: BILLING CYCLE AND PAYMENT
====================================================================

1.1 BILLING CYCLE
NovaTel bills postpaid customers on a monthly cycle. The billing date is 
set on the day the customer activates their account. For example, if a 
customer activates on March 15, their billing cycle runs from the 15th 
of each month to the 14th of the following month.

Bills are generated 3 days after the cycle ends. Payment is due 21 days 
after the bill is generated. For example:
- Cycle ends: April 14
- Bill generated: April 17
- Payment due: May 8

1.2 ACCEPTED PAYMENT METHODS
- Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Debit card (Visa Debit, Interac)
- Pre-authorized bank withdrawal (PAW) - direct debit from chequing account
- NovaTel account credit (from bill credits, returned payments, etc.)
- In-store cash or debit payment at any NovaTel retail location
- Online banking bill payment (allow 3-5 business days to process)

IMPORTANT: NovaTel does NOT accept payment via:
- PayPal
- Cryptocurrency
- Money orders
- Cheques (discontinued as of July 2025)

1.3 AUTOPAY
- Customers can set up AutoPay via My NovaTel app or website
- AutoPay processes payment on the due date
- If AutoPay fails, customer receives SMS and email notification
- System retries payment 3 days after initial failure
- If retry fails, account enters Pre-Suspension status (see Section 3)
- AutoPay discount: $5/month off any postpaid plan when enrolled

SECTION 2: FEES AND CHARGES
====================================================================

2.1 ONE-TIME FEES
- SIM card: $10 (waived for new activations on Essential plan or higher)
- eSIM activation: Free
- SIM replacement (lost/damaged): $10
- Number change: $25
- Account transfer (change account holder): $50
- Paper bill fee: $3/month (waived if customer opts for e-billing)
- Reconnection fee after suspension: $35
- Early device return restocking fee: $75

2.2 LATE PAYMENT FEES
- Late payment fee: $10 applied if payment not received by due date
- Late payment fees are applied once per billing cycle, not daily
- Interest on overdue balances: 2% per month (26.82% annually)
- Interest begins accruing 30 days after the original due date
- Late payment fees can be waived ONE TIME per 12-month period as a 
  courtesy. This must be noted in the account under "Courtesy Credits"

2.3 OVERAGE CHARGES
- Data overage: No overage charges. Data is throttled or paused based 
  on plan type (see Plans Guide for throttle speeds per plan)
- International calls without add-on: $1.50/minute outgoing, 
  $1.00/minute incoming (when roaming)
- International texts without add-on: $0.30/message sent, 
  free to receive
- Premium text services (short codes): $0.50 per message unless 
  specifically blocked on account
- Directory assistance (411): $2.50 per call

SECTION 3: ACCOUNT SUSPENSION AND RECONNECTION
====================================================================

3.1 NON-PAYMENT SUSPENSION TIMELINE
Day 0: Payment due date
Day 1-7: Grace period. Customer receives daily SMS reminders.
Day 8: Late payment fee ($10) applied.
Day 14: Pre-Suspension notice sent (SMS + email). Customer can still 
        make/receive calls but a recorded message plays before each call.
Day 21: OUTGOING services suspended. Customer can still receive calls 
        and texts. Incoming calls play a message: "This NovaTel customer 
        is temporarily unavailable." Data services suspended.
Day 30: FULL suspension. All services suspended including incoming calls. 
        Customer's number is reserved for 90 days.
Day 120: Account cancelled. Phone number released to the number pool. 
         Outstanding balance sent to collections agency.

3.2 RECONNECTION
- Customer must pay all outstanding balances including late fees
- Reconnection fee: $35
- Service restored within 2 hours of payment processing
- If account was fully cancelled (after Day 120), a new account must 
  be created. Original phone number may not be available.

3.3 VOLUNTARY SUSPENSION (SEASONAL HOLD)
- Customers can voluntarily suspend their account for up to 3 months
- Monthly fee during suspension: $15/month (keeps number and account active)
- No data, calls, or text during suspension
- Device financing payments continue during suspension
- Request must be made at least 5 business days before desired start date
- Maximum 2 voluntary suspensions per calendar year

SECTION 4: REFUND POLICY
====================================================================

4.1 GENERAL REFUND RULES
- Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days
- Refunds are returned to the original payment method
- If original payment method is no longer valid, a cheque will be mailed
- Refund requests over $200 require manager approval (escalate to Team Lead)

4.2 ELIGIBLE REFUNDS
- Duplicate payments: Full refund of duplicate amount
- Service outage credits: Prorated credit for outages lasting more than 
  4 continuous hours. Credit = (monthly plan fee / 30) x number of full 
  days affected. Maximum credit: one full month's plan fee.
- Unauthorized charges: Full refund after investigation (7-14 business days). 
  Customer must report unauthorized charges within 90 days.
- Plan change errors: If a customer was moved to wrong plan due to agent 
  error, full refund of any price difference. Escalate to Quality team.
- Device return within buyer's remorse period: Full refund minus $75 
  restocking fee (see Section 5).

4.3 NON-REFUNDABLE CHARGES
- Used data add-ons (even partial use is considered "used")
- International roaming charges for confirmed usage
- Late payment fees (can be waived as courtesy, but not refunded if paid)
- SIM card fees
- Account transfer fees
- Services used during the buyer's remorse period

====================================================================
SECTION 5: BUYER'S REMORSE / RETURN POLICY
====================================================================

5.1 POSTPAID SERVICES
- New customers may cancel within 15 days of activation (the 
  "Buyer's Remorse" period, as mandated by CRTC Wireless Code)
- Customer must return any subsidized or financed device in original 
  condition with all accessories
- Usage limits during buyer's remorse: If customer exceeds the greater 
  of 50% of the plan's monthly usage limit OR 50% of their overage cap, 
  the return privilege is voided
- Refund: All plan charges refunded. Activation fee refunded. Device 
  charges refunded minus $75 restocking fee if device opened.

5.2 DEVICE RETURNS (NON-BUYER'S REMORSE)
- Devices purchased outright can be returned within 15 days if UNOPENED
- Opened devices cannot be returned unless defective
- Defective devices: Exchange or refund within 30 days. After 30 days, 
  warranty claim must be processed through manufacturer.

5.3 PREPAID REFUNDS
- Unused prepaid balances are non-refundable
- Exception: If NovaTel cancels the customer's prepaid account due to 
  a NovaTel error, the remaining balance will be refunded

SECTION 6: CREDIT CHECKS AND DEPOSITS
====================================================================

6.1 CREDIT CHECK POLICY
- All new postpaid activations require a credit check
- Credit check performed via Equifax or TransUnion
- Customer must consent to credit check (verbal consent is sufficient, 
  must be noted in account)
- Prepaid plans do NOT require a credit check

6.2 CREDIT CLASSIFICATIONS
Class A (Excellent Credit): No deposit required. Full device financing 
available. All plans available.

Class B (Good Credit): No deposit required. Device financing up to 
$1,000. All plans available.

Class C (Fair Credit): Deposit of $200 required OR prepaid plan only. 
Device financing up to $400 with deposit.

Class D (Poor Credit/No Credit History): Deposit of $400 required OR 
prepaid plan only. No device financing available.

6.3 DEPOSIT RULES
- Deposits are held for 12 months of consecutive on-time payments
- After 12 months of good standing, deposit is applied as a bill credit
- If account is cancelled before 12 months, deposit is applied to 
  final balance. Any remainder is refunded.
- Deposits do not earn interest

SECTION 7: ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT
====================================================================

7.1 AUTHORIZED USERS
- Account holder can add up to 3 authorized users
- Authorized users can: make plan changes, add features, view bills, 
  make payments
- Authorized users CANNOT: cancel the account, transfer account 
  ownership, or access credit information
- Authorization requires: Full name, date of birth, and a 4-digit PIN 
  set by the account holder

7.2 ACCOUNT SECURITY
- All account access requires identity verification:
  Option 1: Account PIN (4-digit number set at activation)
  Option 2: Security questions (2 of 3 must be answered correctly)
  Option 3: One-time verification code sent to phone on account
- For sensitive operations (cancellation, ownership transfer, port-out), 
  a valid government-issued photo ID is required
- After 3 failed verification attempts, account is locked for 1 hour
- If customer has forgotten all verification methods, they must visit 
  a NovaTel store with government-issued photo ID

7.3 PORT-IN AND PORT-OUT
Port-In (customer bringing number from another carrier):
- Process takes 2-4 hours during business hours
- Customer must provide: Current carrier account number, phone number, 
  and any port-out PIN from current carrier
- Customer should NOT cancel with current carrier before initiating 
  port — the port process automatically cancels the old account

Port-Out (customer leaving NovaTel):
- NovaTel must process port-out requests within 2 business hours 
  (CRTC requirement)
- All outstanding balances remain payable
- Device financing balances become due immediately upon port-out
- Customer should contact new carrier to initiate the port-out

SECTION 8: TAXES AND REGULATORY FEES
====================================================================

All NovaTel prices are shown BEFORE tax. The following taxes and fees 
are added to every bill:

Federal:
- GST (Goods and Services Tax): 5%

Provincial (varies by province):
- Ontario: HST 13% (replaces GST)
- Quebec: GST 5% + QST 9.975%
- British Columbia: GST 5% + PST 7%
- Alberta: GST 5% only
- Other provinces: See provincial tax table in Knowledge Base

Regulatory:
- 911 Emergency Services Fee: $0.75/month per line
- CRTC Contribution Levy: Included in plan price (not shown separately)

IMPORTANT: Taxes are NOT included in advertised plan prices. When 
quoting plan costs to customers, always clarify: "The plan is $XX per 
month plus applicable taxes."
