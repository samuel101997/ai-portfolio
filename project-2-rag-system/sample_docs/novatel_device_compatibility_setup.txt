SECTION 1: NETWORK TECHNOLOGY AND BANDS
====================================================================

1.1 NOVATEL NETWORK OVERVIEW
NovaTel Mobile operates on the following network technologies:
- 5G NR (New Radio): Primary network for data and voice in urban areas
- 4G LTE: Nationwide coverage backbone
- 3G UMTS/HSPA+: Legacy network - SCHEDULED FOR SHUTDOWN JULY 1, 2026
  IMPORTANT: After July 1, 2026, devices that only support 3G will no 
  longer work on the NovaTel network. Proactively inform customers with 
  3G-only devices.

1.2 REQUIRED FREQUENCY BANDS
For full compatibility with NovaTel's network, devices must support:

4G LTE Bands (minimum required marked with *):
- Band 2 (1900 MHz)* - Primary LTE band
- Band 4 (AWS-1 1700/2100 MHz)* - Primary LTE band
- Band 5 (850 MHz) - Extended rural coverage
- Band 7 (2600 MHz)* - Urban high-capacity
- Band 12 (700 MHz) - Building penetration
- Band 13 (700 MHz) - Extended coverage
- Band 17 (700 MHz) - Building penetration
- Band 66 (AWS-3) - Extended AWS coverage

5G NR Bands:
- n2 (1900 MHz) - 5G coverage layer
- n5 (850 MHz) - 5G low-band coverage
- n7 (2600 MHz) - 5G mid-band
- n12 (700 MHz) - 5G low-band
- n66 (AWS) - 5G coverage
- n77 (3.5 GHz) - 5G high-capacity (primary 5G band)
- n78 (3.5 GHz) - 5G high-capacity

A device must support at minimum Band 2, Band 4, and Band 7 for LTE 
to be considered compatible. 5G access requires 5G-capable device AND 
a 5G-enabled plan (all current plans include 5G access).

SECTION 2: BYOD (BRING YOUR OWN DEVICE)
====================================================================

2.1 COMPATIBILITY CHECK PROCESS
Before activating a customer's own device:
1) Obtain the device IMEI (customer dials *#06# or checks Settings > 
   About Phone)
2) Enter IMEI into the NovaTel IMEI Checker tool
3) The tool will report:
   - "Fully Compatible" - All bands supported, proceed with activation
   - "Partially Compatible" - Some bands missing, may experience 
     coverage gaps in certain areas. Inform customer before proceeding.
   - "Not Compatible" - Critical bands missing. Do NOT activate. 
     Recommend device upgrade or NovaTel-certified device.
   - "Blocked" - Device is on the national blocked device list (lost, 
     stolen, or involved in fraud). Do NOT activate. If customer claims 
     ownership, advise them to contact their previous carrier.

2.2 DEVICE LOCK STATUS
- All NovaTel devices sold after December 1, 2017 are sold UNLOCKED 
  (CRTC mandate)
- For devices purchased before that date, NovaTel will unlock free of 
  charge upon request. Use the Device Unlock tool in the agent portal.
- BYOD devices must be unlocked before activation on NovaTel. If a 
  customer's device is locked to another carrier, they must contact 
  that carrier to unlock it first.

SECTION 3: SIM CARDS AND eSIM
====================================================================

3.1 PHYSICAL SIM CARDS
NovaTel uses a universal SIM card that can be popped out to three sizes:
- Standard SIM (1FF) - Rarely used, legacy devices only
- Micro SIM (3FF) - Older smartphones and some tablets
- Nano SIM (4FF) - Most modern smartphones

SIM Card SKUs:
- NV-SIM-001: Universal SIM (new activation) - $10
- NV-SIM-002: Universal SIM (replacement) - $10
- NV-SIM-003: Universal SIM (device upgrade, same number) - Free

SIM cards are available at all NovaTel retail stores and can be shipped 
to the customer's address (delivery in 3-5 business days, free shipping).

3.2 eSIM (EMBEDDED SIM)
NovaTel supports eSIM activation on compatible devices.

eSIM Supported Devices (as of January 2026):
Apple: iPhone XS and later, iPad Pro (3rd gen and later), iPad Air 
(3rd gen and later), Apple Watch Series 4 and later
Samsung: Galaxy S20 and later, Galaxy Z Flip/Fold series, Galaxy 
Watch4 and later
Google: Pixel 3a and later
Other: Most flagship devices from 2020 onward - check IMEI tool

eSIM Activation Process:
1) Verify device eSIM compatibility via IMEI Checker
2) Generate eSIM profile in the eSIM Management tool
3) Provide customer with QR code (via email or display on agent screen)
4) Customer scans QR code: Settings > Cellular > Add eSIM > 
   Scan QR Code
5) eSIM activates within 5 minutes
6) Confirm activation by having customer make a test call

eSIM Key Rules:
- One eSIM profile per NovaTel line (cannot use same number on multiple 
  eSIM devices simultaneously)
- eSIM can coexist with a physical SIM from another carrier (dual SIM)
- eSIM profiles can be transferred between devices by generating a new 
  QR code (old profile is automatically deactivated)
- eSIM activation fee: Free
- No physical SIM is needed when using eSIM

SECTION 4: DEVICE SETUP - ANDROID
====================================================================

4.1 INITIAL SETUP FOR NOVATEL ON ANDROID
Step 1: Insert NovaTel SIM card or activate eSIM
Step 2: Power on the device and follow on-screen setup wizard
Step 3: When prompted for mobile network settings, the device should 
        automatically detect NovaTel's network and configure settings
Step 4: If automatic configuration fails, manually set APN:
        Settings > Network & Internet > Mobile Network > Access Point 
        Names > (+) Add new APN
        Enter the following:
        - Name: NovaTel Internet
        - APN: novatel.data
        - MMSC: http://mms.novatel.ca
        - MMS Proxy: mmsproxy.novatel.ca
        - MMS Port: 8080
        - MCC: 302
        - MNC: 880
        - APN Type: default,mms,supl
        - APN Protocol: IPv4/IPv6
        Save and select the NovaTel Internet APN

Step 5: Enable VoLTE for HD voice calls:
        Settings > Network & Internet > Mobile Network > 
        VoLTE / HD Voice > Enable

Step 6: Enable Wi-Fi Calling (optional):
        Settings > Network & Internet > Mobile Network > 
        Wi-Fi Calling > Enable
        Set preference: "Wi-Fi preferred" or "Cellular preferred"

Step 7: Set up voicemail:
        Dial *86 and follow voice prompts to set up PIN and greeting

Step 8: Download the My NovaTel app from Google Play Store for 
        account management, data usage tracking, and bill payment

SECTION 5: DEVICE SETUP - iPHONE
====================================================================

5.1 INITIAL SETUP FOR NOVATEL ON iPHONE
Step 1: Insert NovaTel SIM card or activate eSIM
Step 2: Power on and follow on-screen setup wizard
Step 3: iPhone should automatically download NovaTel carrier settings
        (requires Wi-Fi or data connection)
Step 4: If carrier settings do not download automatically:
        Settings > General > About > If prompted, tap "Update"
        If no prompt appears, check Settings > General > About and 
        verify "Carrier" shows "NovaTel" with a version number

Step 5: Verify cellular settings:
        Settings > Cellular > Cellular Data Options:
        - Enable LTE: Voice & Data
        - Data Roaming: OFF (unless customer has roaming add-on)

Step 6: Enable Wi-Fi Calling:
        Settings > Phone > Wi-Fi Calling > Enable
        Enter emergency address (required by regulation)

Step 7: Visual Voicemail setup:
        Phone app > Voicemail tab > Set Up Now
        Create voicemail password and record greeting

Step 8: Download the My NovaTel app from the App Store

5.2 iPHONE-SPECIFIC KNOWN ISSUES
- iOS 18.x: Some customers report intermittent 5G connection drops. 
  Workaround: Settings > Cellular > Cellular Data Options > 
  Voice & Data > set to "5G Auto" instead of "5G On"
- Dual SIM (eSIM + Physical): If customer uses NovaTel eSIM with 
  another carrier's physical SIM, ensure NovaTel is set as the 
  default line for cellular data
- iMessage/FaceTime: After SIM swap or eSIM activation, customer may 
  need to toggle iMessage OFF then ON to re-register their number

SECTION 6: DEVICE FINANCING
====================================================================

6.1 NOVATEL DEVICE FINANCING PROGRAM
NovaTel offers 0% interest device financing on eligible plans:
- Starter plan: Not eligible
- Essential plan: Up to $500 financing
- Premium plan: Up to $1,200 financing
- Ultimate plan: Up to $2,000 financing
- Prepaid plans: Not eligible

Financing Terms:
- 24-month term (monthly installments added to phone bill)
- 0% interest for the full term
- Early payoff allowed at any time with no penalty
- If customer cancels service or ports out, remaining device balance 
  becomes due immediately
- If customer downgrades to an ineligible plan, device financing 
  continues but no further financing is available

6.2 DEVICE UPGRADE PROGRAM (TRADE-IN)
Customers with active device financing can upgrade early:
- After 12 months: Trade in current device, remaining balance waived 
  up to $300. Customer starts new financing on new device.
- After 18 months: Trade in current device, remaining balance fully 
  waived. Customer starts new financing on new device.
- Before 12 months: Must pay remaining balance in full to upgrade.

Trade-in conditions:
- Device must power on
- Screen must not be cracked or shattered
- No water damage indicators triggered
- Device must not be on the blocked device list
- All personal data must be factory reset before trade-in

SECTION 7: WEARABLES AND TABLETS
====================================================================

7.1 SMARTWATCH PLANS
NovaTel supports cellular smartwatches with a paired plan:
- Smartwatch Add-On: $10/month per watch
- Shares the primary line's phone number
- Requires compatible watch (Apple Watch Series 4+, Samsung Galaxy 
  Watch4+, Google Pixel Watch)
- Includes: Unlimited talk/text, 500 MB data
- Additional watch data: $5 per 500 MB add-on

Setup: Pair watch with phone using manufacturer's app. In the app, 
select "Set up cellular" and choose NovaTel when prompted. Activation 
completes automatically.

7.2 TABLET PLANS
NovaTel offers standalone data plans for tablets:
- Tablet 5 GB: $15/month
- Tablet 15 GB: $25/month
- Tablet Unlimited: $40/month (throttled after 30 GB)

Tablet plans are data-only. No voice or SMS capability. Tablet plans 
can be added as additional lines on an existing postpaid account 
(multi-line discounts apply).
